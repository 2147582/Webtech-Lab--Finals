Roller, 3-5 Ton, Double Drum Smooth
Suitable for use on asphalt and soil/gravel sub-bases, double drum rollers are ideal for driveways, parking lots, patching, curb, and road work. Hydrostatic drive gives you smooth, even acceleration and stopping to help prevent asphalt marring. Speed control is infinitely variable. Dual drum hydraulic drive and articulated steering provide excellent performance and confined area maneuverability. Pressurized water sprinkler system and two scrapers per drum are standard to keep drums clean. Operator stations offer excellent visibility and vibration cushioned seats. All 40" to 48" models feature dual drum vibration.
